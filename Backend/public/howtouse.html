How to Use WarrantyVault
Follow these simple steps to manage your warranties efficiently

1

Explore the Home Page

After opening WarrantyVault, you'll land on the home page. Use the navigation bar to access Features, Blog, How to Use, or go directly to your Dashboard.
Tip: Click "Go to Dashboard" for quick access to your active warranties.
2

Dashboard Overview

View all your products, warranty statuses, and quick actions. Stats show total, active, expiring soon, and expired warranties in a clean interface.
Use the "Add Product" button to register a new product instantly.
3

Add a New Product

Fill in product details, upload the product image, invoice, and warranty card. This keeps all your documents securely organized in one place.
You can download invoices and warranty cards anytime from the product page.
4

View Details & Claim

Click on any product to view its details. Access warranty info, download documents, and claim warranty directly via the external link.
Use the "Visit Warranty Page" button to jump to the manufacturer's claim portal.
5

Check Warranty Status

For supported brands, you can check warranty status by entering the serial number on their official portal via our quick links.
Always keep your serial number handy when contacting support.
